Code of Conduct Policy

Purpose

The Code of Conduct Policy aims to uphold Villa Roma Hotel's commitment to professionalism, integrity, and excellence. This policy provides clear behavioral and ethical guidelines to ensure a safe, respectful, and welcoming environment for all employees, trainees, students, and guests.

Scope

This policy applies to all Villa Roma Hotel employees, students from Nkangala TVET College participating in the training program, and any third-party contractors or vendors working on-site.

Policy Guidelines

Professionalism and Integrity

  • Honesty and Fairness: All employees and trainees are expected to act honestly and fairly in all interactions, both internally and with guests.
  • Ethical Behavior: Avoid conflicts of interest, report unethical practices, and maintain integrity in all professional activities.
  • Accountability: Each individual is responsible for their actions and should complete tasks diligently, reliably, and promptly.

Respect and Inclusivity

  • Non-Discrimination: Villa Roma is committed to providing a work and training environment free from discrimination on the basis of race, gender, religion, nationality, disability, or any other protected characteristic.
  • Harassment-Free Workplace: Harassment, bullying, or any form of intimidation is strictly prohibited. This includes verbal, physical, and online behaviors.
  • Courtesy and Respect: Employees and trainees are expected to communicate courteously and respectfully with all colleagues, guests, and management.

Confidentiality and Data Protection

  • Guest Privacy: Information about guests, including personal, financial, and booking details, is confidential and should be handled in compliance with privacy regulations.
  • Confidential Information: Employees and trainees must not disclose any proprietary information, including hotel procedures, pricing, or operational data, without authorization.

Appearance and Personal Hygiene

  • Uniform and Dress Code: Employees and trainees must adhere to the established dress code for their roles, ensuring they present a polished, professional appearance. Uniforms should be clean and well-maintained.
  • Personal Hygiene: Maintain high personal hygiene standards, especially in guest-facing roles, and follow health guidelines specific to food handling or housekeeping roles.

Health, Safety, and Environment

  • Compliance with Safety Standards: Follow all safety procedures, report any hazards or unsafe conditions, and participate in safety training as required.
  • Substance-Free Workplace: Villa Roma Hotel maintains a substance-free workplace. Consumption of alcohol or illegal substances on duty is prohibited.
  • Commitment to Sustainability: Staff and trainees are encouraged to support Villa Roma's sustainability initiatives, including waste reduction, energy conservation, and recycling practices.

Workplace Conduct and Guest Service

  • Excellence in Guest Service: Employees and trainees are expected to provide friendly, attentive, and personalized service, going above and beyond to enhance the guest experience.
  • Team Collaboration: Work cooperatively with colleagues to promote a positive and productive environment. Support team members when needed and share knowledge and skills generously.
  • Conflict Resolution: Address conflicts constructively and professionally. Any unresolved issues should be escalated to a supervisor or the HR department.

Use of Property and Resources

  • Care of Hotel Property: Employees and trainees are responsible for the proper use and care of all hotel equipment, furniture, and resources.
  • Personal Devices: Limit personal device use (e.g., cell phones) during work hours to emergencies or designated break times.
  • Responsible Resource Use: Minimize waste by using resources like water, energy, and supplies efficiently.

Technology and Social Media Use

  • Internet and IT Security: Employees must comply with Villa Roma's IT policies, avoiding unauthorized access or misuse of hotel systems.
  • Social Media Conduct: Employees and trainees are discouraged from sharing hotel information on personal social media. If representing Villa Roma online, exercise professionalism and discretion.
  • Data Security: Do not use or share any confidential information without authorization, particularly information related to guests or financial data.

Compliance and Disciplinary Action

  • Compliance Monitoring: Villa Roma management will actively monitor compliance with this policy through supervision, guest feedback, and routine audits.
  • Reporting Violations: Any violations or breaches of this policy should be reported immediately to a supervisor, manager, or HR department. Employees and trainees reporting issues in good faith will be protected from retaliation.
  • Disciplinary Measures: Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in disciplinary actions, including verbal or written warnings, suspension, termination, or other actions based on the severity of the violation.

Policy Review and Updates

This Code of Conduct Policy will be reviewed annually to ensure it reflects the latest best practices and legal requirements. Amendments to this policy will be communicated to all employees and trainees through official channels.

Acknowledgment

All employees and trainees are required to read, understand, and sign this Code of Conduct upon joining Villa Roma Hotel. Failure to adhere to these standards may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment or training.
